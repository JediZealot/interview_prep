10 Probable General Interview Questions





**Motivation**: "Why are you interested in M\&A analytics specifically, and why Deloitte?"



**Career Goals:** "Where do you see yourself in 3-5 years, and how does this internship fit into your career trajectory?"



**M\&A Understanding:** "What do you understand about the M\&A deal cycle, and where do you think data analytics adds the most value?"



**Challenging Project:** "Tell me about your most challenging project. What obstacles did you face and how did you overcome them?"



**Team Collaboration:** "Describe a time when you had to work with a difficult team member. How did you handle it?"



**Pressure Handling:** "M\&A deals often have tight deadlines. How do you manage stress and prioritize tasks under time pressure?"



**Client Focus:** "Give an example of when you had to explain complex technical concepts to a non-technical audience."



**Ethical Dilemma:** "How would you handle a situation where you discovered concerning data during due diligence?"



**Learning Agility:** "Tell me about a time you had to quickly learn a new technology or skill for a project."



**Cultural Fit:** "What does diversity and inclusion mean to you, and how have you contributed to it in academic or professional settings?"
